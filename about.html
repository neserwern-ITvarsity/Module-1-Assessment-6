About our company

Nes Boots is a newly formed company,selling Dr Martens which is a British brand of boots and shoes known for their distinctive air-cushioned soles, yellow stitching, and welted construction, initially created by Dr. Klaus Maertens in 1945 using discarded military materials for comfort after a skiing
accident. The brand gained popularity with working-class subcultures like skinheads and punk rockers, evolving from a work boot into a symbol of self-expression and a staple in various youth cultures globally.
